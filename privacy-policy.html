Privacy Policy for Sortedly

Effective Date: December 6, 2024

Overview

Sortedly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Sortedly mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Tasks and Reminders: Task names, descriptions, due dates, categories, and completion status
  • Shopping Lists: Item names, quantities, categories, and purchase status
  • Subscriptions and Policies: Service names, amounts, renewal dates, and related details
  • App Settings: Theme preferences, notification settings, and other app configurations

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version (only for technical support)
  • Usage Data: App crash reports and error logs (to improve app stability)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide core app functionality (tasks, shopping lists, subscriptions management)
  • Send local notifications and reminders
  • Backup and restore your data when you choose to do so
  • Provide technical support when you contact us

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All your personal data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not store your personal information on external servers
  • Your data remains under your control at all times

Data Backup

  • Backup functionality allows you to export your data to your chosen location
  • Backups are created and stored by you, not by us
  • We do not have access to your backup files

Security Measures

  • Data is stored using your device's secure storage systems
  • We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information
  • No data transmission to external servers occurs without your explicit action

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to third parties.

Your data may only be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • When you explicitly choose to backup or export your data
  • When you contact us for support (only information you choose to share)
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights

Notifications

  • Local Notifications: We use your device's notification system to remind you about tasks
  • Notification Permissions: You can enable or disable notifications in the app settings
  • No External Communication: Notifications are generated locally on your device

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Modify: Edit or update your information at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual items or reset all data
  • Export: Create backups of your data
  • Control: Manage notification settings and app preferences

Children's Privacy

Sortedly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will notify you through the app or by email
  • The updated policy will be effective immediately upon posting
  • Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

Third-Party Services

Sortedly does not integrate with third-party services that collect your personal information. All functionality is provided locally on your device.

Data Retention

  • Your data is retained locally on your device until you choose to delete it
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data
  • We do not retain copies of your data on external servers

International Users

If you are using Sortedly outside of your country of residence, please be aware that your information may be processed in the country where you use the app. By using Sortedly, you consent to such processing.

Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws
